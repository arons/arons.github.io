My keyboards

26 Set 2021

I want to share some thoughts and stuff I've done with my keyboards.

Some time ago I wanted to buy a new keyboard and since I spend a lot of time with it I think is worth to invest money on it. Also I decided to go for a Mechanical one. I'm not really sure why. I think just because, once you start searching about keyboard on Internet, a new world open to you and you get lost in it. After some search, I found out that a lot of people are obsessed by mechanical keyboard and I became really curious to try one.

Because I like also DIY, I of course checked how it would be to build your own from scratch and realize that was not really interesting to me, it looks to me quite time-consuming and really expensive.

I decide then to first check if I like mechanical keyboard and I found quite good reviews about the Durgod K32. I think I bought from Amazon.

It is a really simple keyboard, I choose the one without backlight and with red silent switches. I really like the keyboard and I'm still using it at home.

After than one, cause I fall in love with mechanical keyboard, was a turn to buy another one for my work place. I opt of the Drop CTRL. That because on that model is possible to change the switch at any time without desoldering them.

Next step was to update both keyboards with same layout. In order to do that I needed to install qmk_firmware on my durgod (the drop already running the qmk_firmware), you can find the instruction to flash the firmware here.

I'm really happy with this setup. The durgod with red silence is the best for home working, especially during night session, it is really quiet.
